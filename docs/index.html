ℹ️ How macros are calculated

Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula for BMR × an activity multiplier for TDEE, then applies your goal's calorie adjustment based on your current logged weight. Protein is set to about 1g per lb of current bodyweight, fat to 25% of calories, and carbs fill the rest. This is a solid, widely-used estimate — not personalized medical or dietitian advice. Adjust based on real-world results after 2–3 weeks.
